Why it exists

A calmer planning layer for real days.

Most routine tools either become rigid checklists or generic calendars. Henge sits between those modes: it gives your day a structure, keeps reminders close, and leaves enough room for review notes and changes.

Storage

Local-first

Your routines, notes, and review history are stored locally on your device unless you export them yourself.

Privacy

Private by design

No account is required. The app does not use third-party advertising or third-party analytics.

Control

Backup ready

You can export and import your app data from the app settings using a JSON backup.

Data ownership

Your planning data stays under your control.

Henge does not require an account, does not use third-party advertising, and does not use third-party analytics. Backup files are created only when you choose to export them.
