Bug reports

What to include

When reporting a bug, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of what happened. Screenshots are useful when the issue is visual.

  • Device model
  • iOS version
  • Henge app version
  • Steps that caused the issue

Common questions

Fast answers

Where is my data stored?

Your data is stored locally on your device.

Can I back up my data?

Yes. Open the app settings and use Export Backup to save a JSON backup. You can later import that file from the same settings area.

Why are notifications not appearing?

Make sure notifications are enabled for Henge in iOS Settings and inside the app settings.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete local app data from the app settings. You can also delete the app from your device to remove its local data.
